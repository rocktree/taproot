What's happening with Sapwood?

Sapwood is young. It is a small child in the vast world of CMS-like applications. It's changing significantly on a regular basis as we learn what people need to make it work for them.

The articles here are meant to help keep you up to date with what's going on with Sapwood.

05.13.15

v1.5 Small, But Overdue

The media library needed some attention, and we have a few other hidden gems to offer in v1.5!

05.12.15

WARNING! Docs Are Out Of Date

The docs have fallen behind all the awesome features in Sapwood, but they will be updated soon.

04.10.15

v1.4 Introduces Menus and Error Tracking

Static menus were becoming a headache, so now we offer the option to build custom menus through the builder, along with some other cool features.

04.02.15

v1.3 Makes Blogging a Little Easier

v1.3 adds several features geared towards making Sapwood work for blogging purposes.

v1.2 Brings Major Workflow Enhancements

While v1.1 made the guts of page types and templates work better, v1.2 focus on the workflow through the UI.

03.09.15

Roadmap Publicly Available

The roadmap provides an ability to see where Sapwood is headed over the next several months.

02.19.15

v1.1 Consolidates Page Types and Templates

Sapwood v1.1 consolidates the ideas of page types and templates, alters the communicative workflow, and adds some developer helpers.
